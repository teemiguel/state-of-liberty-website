Our Mission

LiftASmile provides children in underserved communities with access to quality education, creative arts programs, and mentorship opportunities. We believe creativity is a fundamental right and a powerful tool for social change.

Founded in 2024, we've already impacted over 2,500 children across Cape Town and Johannesburg, fostering confidence, skills, and joy through hands-on workshops and community events.
