Who We Are

State of Liberty is a modern talent agency founded on one core belief: creativity thrives where there is freedom.

We represent diverse talent across modelling, acting, music, performing arts, and creative industries — giving them a platform to grow, evolve, and be fully themselves.

Our Mission

To empower talent with the freedom, opportunities, and support they need to grow boldly and authentically in the creative industry.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s leading creative hub where individuality is celebrated and talent is transformed into global excellence.
